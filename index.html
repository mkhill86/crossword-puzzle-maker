Make your own Word Search Puzzle!

Following these directions to create your own custom word search puzzle

1. Press Start

Type in any word you want into the word search puzzle box. Make sure to also list your word under the Word List section.

2. Press Fill

This will freeze all your chosen words and then fill the empty spaces with random letters.

3. Press Reset

Clears the board and now it's time to make a new one!
